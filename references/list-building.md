# List Building and First-Party Data

## Pop-ups (Stroud)

Custom pop-ups in Klaviyo based on traffic source (UTM codes), ad campaign, demographics. Pop-up messaging mirrors the VP of the ad that brought them. Target 5% capture minimum, 8-12% for matched audiences.

## Co-Registration (Stroud)

Partner with influencers for lead capture on their blogs/sites. Co-brand the pop-up, add leads to customized nurture. 11-16% capture rates reported.

## First-Party Data Enrichment

- Zero-party data: post-purchase surveys, quizzes, preference centers. "What brought you here?" and "What are you looking for?" are high-value questions.
- CLV-based segmentation: segment by predicted LTV, not just engagement. High-CLV gets priority. Only ~20% of Klaviyo accounts use this but those that do see 15-25% higher RPR.
- Event-based profiles: track browse, ATC, purchase, return events in Klaviyo for behavioral profiles.
