# High-Ticket Strategy (Long Consideration Cycles)

For products where customers don't purchase within Meta's 7-day attribution window ($200+ AOV, luxury, B2B).

Purchase optimization fails because the algorithm has nothing to learn from. Nobody converts in-window.

## The Play

- Identify what high-intent users DO within 7 days: view specific pages 60+ seconds, submit inquiry forms, book appointments, request quotes, download lookbooks, wishlist.
- Create a custom conversion event for that action.
- Optimize TOF for the custom event. Hits 25-50 events/week more easily.
- Convert off-platform: email nurture, personal outreach, in-store, phone. Timeline: 45-90 days.
- Metrics: cost per qualified lead (custom event), close rate and revenue via CRM. Platform ROAS will always look bad.

Under Andromeda: diverse creative in one ad set, optimize for custom event, broad targeting.

## Retargeting for High-Ticket

Email and personal outreach do the retargeting job, not paid ads.

Exception: if 5,000+ monthly site visitors, run low-budget brand reinforcement campaign (behind-the-scenes, press, social proof). Optimize for reach, cap frequency 1-2x/week. Not "buy now" ads.
