|---|---|---|---|

| Thumbstop ratio | 20-30% | 35%+ | Meta 2026 |
| Cost per ATC | <15% of AOV | <10% of AOV | Stroud |
| Cart-to-detail rate | 6-9% | 10%+ | Stroud |
| Buy-to-detail rate | 22-48% | 50%+ | Stroud |

## Email (Klaviyo 2026)

| Metric | Target | Top Performers | Source |
|---|---|---|---|
| Pop-up email capture rate | 5% | 8-12% | Stroud/Klaviyo |
| Welcome flow open rate | 35-45% | 45-50% | Klaviyo 2026 |
| Welcome flow conversion rate | 8-12% | 12-18% | Klaviyo 2026 |
| Cart abandonment open rate | 30-40% | 40%+ | Klaviyo 2026 |
| Cart abandonment recovery rate | 5-8% | 8-12% | Klaviyo 2026 |

## Key Notes

- Hook rate and thumbstop ratio are better creative quality indicators than CTR. CTR is secondary.
- Creative fatigue indicators: frequency exceeds 3.0 AND CTR drops 30%+.
- Flows generate 41% of total email revenue from 5.3% of sends. RPR for flows is ~18x higher than campaigns.
