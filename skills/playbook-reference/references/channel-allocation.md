# Channel Allocation

Don't use fixed percentage ratios. Use incrementality data.

## Seasonal Budget (Stroud)

- High season (40% of year): 60-70% of annual budget
- Evergreen (30-40%): steady-state, list building and flows
- Low season: reduce paid, focus organic and retention

## Channel Roles

- Meta: prospecting and demand generation. Best for TOF with video. Increase when creative testing shows strong hooks and you have a proven MAT message.
- Google: demand capture. Search, Shopping, PMax for high-intent. Increase when branded search volume grows (signal TOF is working).
- TikTok: awareness and virality. Raw native > polished. Increase for 18-34 when you have UGC/creator content.
- Amazon: bottom-funnel capture. Increase when organic rank is near page 1 or during seasonal spikes.
- Email/SMS: retention and conversion. Highest ROI for existing customers. Always on.
