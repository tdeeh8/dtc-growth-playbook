# /humanize

Apply the Human Voice Protocol to make content sound like a real person wrote it. Eliminates AI linguistic fingerprints, fixes robotic patterns, and produces natural-sounding copy.

## What It Does

1. Scans content for AI tells (banned words, phrases, structural patterns)
2. Replaces Tier 1 and Tier 2 AI signal words with natural alternatives
3. Fixes punctuation patterns (em dash overuse, semicolons)
4. Breaks up structural tells (identical sentence length, rule-of-three, formulaic paragraphs)
5. Adjusts tone (removes hedging, motivational poster tone, over-explaining)
6. Runs the full self-check checklist before delivering

## Usage

- `/humanize` then paste or describe the content to edit
- Or ask Claude to "make this sound human" / "humanize this" at any point in conversation
- Works on any content type: emails, social posts, blog articles, product descriptions, cover letters, client comms
