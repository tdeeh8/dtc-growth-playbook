# Measurement and Attribution

Platform-reported ROAS is unreliable. Use a three-layer stack.

## The Stack

Attribution: tracks user journeys, assigns credit. Daily optimization. Can't prove causation.
Incrementality: controlled experiments (holdouts, geo-lifts) to prove ads caused sales. Budget allocation decisions. Quarterly.
Media mix modeling: aggregate historical data, cross-channel effects. Strategic/annual planning.

## Practical Steps

- Minimum: cross-validate platform revenue vs. GA4/Shopify. If platform total exceeds Shopify by 30%+, attribution overlap.
- Better: quarterly holdout tests on biggest channel. Turn off ads in 2-3 geos for 2 weeks, measure vs. control.
- Best: dedicated platform (Northbeam, Rockerbox, Triple Whale).

## Reporting Rules

Always state attribution model in every report. "Meta 7-day click, 1-day view" and "Google last-click" are different things. Mixing without noting the difference leads to bad decisions.
