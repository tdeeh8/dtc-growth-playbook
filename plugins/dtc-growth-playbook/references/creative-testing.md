Creative is responsible for ~56% of auction outcomes on Meta.

## Components

Every ad has three parts:
- Hook: first 3 seconds of video or headline/opening image. Needs text overlay, sound hook, visual hook, vibe.
- Body: main message, product demo, narrative. Where the MAT-tested VP lives.
- CTA: closing offer, urgency driver, direct ask.

## Testing Method

3:2:2 (3 hooks, 2 bodies, 2 CTAs) inside a single Dynamic Creative ad set with Advantage+ placements, broad targeting. 12 combinations without 12 separate ads.

After 3:2:2 winners found: isolation testing. Change one variable at a time, document learning, move to next.

## Budget Rule

70% scaling proven winners, 20% structured testing, 10% bold exploration.

## Significance Standards

Minimum 7 days. At least 50 conversions per variant (25 under Andromeda emerging threshold). 95% confidence before calling a winner.
