# Low-Ticket Strategy (Under ~$100 AOV)

Three-layer framework compresses. Short consideration window, impulse buying is real.

## How Layers Shift

Layer 1 (Reach/ThruPlay): still essential. Only reliable way to get new eyeballs at low frequency. Without it, purchase campaigns recycle the same pool. For brand new accounts, run Layer 1 for 2-3 weeks to seed pixel before purchase campaigns.

Layer 2 (ATC): testing tool, not permanent campaign. Use during MAT/creative testing for cheap volume. Once winners found, fold into Layer 3. Gap between ATC and purchase is tiny at this price point.

Layer 3 (Purchase): majority of conversion budget. Broad targeting, 15-25 diverse creatives. Buyer pool is large enough at low price points that most accounts hit 25-50 purchases/week easily.

## Prospecting Approach

Layer 1 (Reach/ThruPlay) for new eyeballs + Layer 3 (Purchase) for conversion. Email/SMS picks up everyone via pop-up capture. Flows do the repetition work.

## Key Bottleneck

Almost always creative fatigue, not audience saturation. Aggressive refresh (every 2-3 weeks for high spend) is more important than audience manipulation.
