# TOF Campaign Strategy — Three-Layer Framework

"Optimize for purchase" tells Meta to find buyers, and buyers are BOF. A purchase campaign collapses toward people already close to buying. Separate the jobs.

## Layer 1: Find New People (Reach/Awareness)

Goal: new people who match your customer profile but aren't in-market yet.

Campaign type: Reach or Video Views (ThruPlay). NOT conversion, traffic, or engagement.
- Strong video content: Video Views (ThruPlay). More engagement signals for Andromeda.
- Mostly static: Reach campaign. Maximum unique reach at lowest CPM.

Targeting: broad. No interests, no lookalikes. Advantage+ placements.
Creative: diverse MAT-tested creative. Creative acts as the filter. 75%+ video viewers are self-selecting as interested.
Video length: 15-30 seconds. ThruPlay counts at 15s, so under 15s gives no view-depth signal. Over 30s drops completion rates.
Frequency: reach campaigns stay low naturally. Manual cap: 1 impression per 7 days for sub-2/month.
Budget: largest share, especially for new clients.
Metrics: hook rate, ThruPlay rate, cost per ThruPlay, unique reach. NOT CPA. That is not Layer 1's job.

## Layer 2: Identify Intent (ATC / Custom Event)

Goal: find people showing real purchase intent through site actions.

Campaign type: ATC or custom conversion event. Broad targeting.
No need to retarget Layer 1 viewers manually. Andromeda routes automatically.
Budget: medium share.
Metrics: cost per ATC, cost per custom event, GA4 quality signals.

## Layer 3: Close (Purchase)

Campaign type: Purchase. Advantage+ with new customer toggle.
Targeting: broad or narrow. Exclude 30-60 day purchasers.
Creative: offer-driven, urgency, scarcity, testimonials.
Budget: smallest share.
Metrics: ROAS, CPA, cost per new customer.

## Retargeting Between Layers

Layer 1 to Layer 2: Andromeda handles automatically. No manual retargeting needed.
Layer 2 to Layer 3: optional. Only if message changes meaningfully for site-action users.

## Budget Ratios by Client Maturity

- New client / limited pixel: 60% L1, 25% L2, 15% L3
- Established / strong data: 40% L1, 30% L2, 30% L3
- Mature / scaling: 30% L1, 30% L2, 40% L3

## Event Selection

Pick the lowest-funnel event you can hit 25-50 of per week per ad set. Most DTC: L2 = ATC, L3 = purchase. Low volume: L2 = view content, L3 = ATC.
