# Meta Andromeda (October 2025 Global Rollout)

Andromeda uses creative to determine who sees the ad. Creative IS targeting.

## Key Rules

- Fewer ad sets, more creatives per ad set. 1 ad set with 25 creatives = 17% more conversions at 16% lower cost vs. 5 ad sets with 5 creatives each.
- Broad targeting delivers 49% higher ROAS vs. lookalikes.
- Need 10-20+ unique creative concepts per campaign.
- Creative diversity = genuinely different hooks, angles, formats, messaging. Slight variations count as identical to Andromeda.
- Healthy mix: 60% new concepts, 40% variants of proven winners.
- Creative fatigue: 10-14 days for high-spend. Refresh hooks every 2-4 weeks.
- Frequency management: solved by creative volume, not exclusions. Andromeda rotates automatically if given enough variety.
- Formats: static images, short-form raw video, founder selfies, polished production, GIFs, memes, carousels.
- Advantage+ placements recommended.

## Learning Phase

Official: 50 conversions/week per ad set. Emerging under Andromeda: ~25/week may be sufficient. Use 50 as safe target, 25 as floor.

## Performance

Advertisers following Andromeda best practices report 20-35% higher ROAS vs. legacy structures.
